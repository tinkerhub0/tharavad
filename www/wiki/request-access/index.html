Request access

tharavad is invite-gated. You sign up through an enrollment form, and an admin approves your account before you can log in.

Step 1 — Open the enrollment form

Visit the signup URL below. This uses Authentik — an open-source identity provider that handles accounts, SSH keys, and auth for the entire tharavad.xyz platform.

https://auth.tharavad.xyz/if/flow/tinkerhub-enrollment/

Fill in your details and submit. You'll land on a confirmation screen once the form is submitted.

Step 2 — Wait for approval

An admin reviews your signup and approves it. There's no automated email — if you're in the TinkerHub Kerala community, ping someone there to get your account approved faster.

Note: You cannot SSH in until your account is approved. The server will reject your connection with "Permission denied" until the admin enables your account.

Step 3 — Log in to the portal

Once approved, you can sign in at auth.tharavad.xyz. From there you can manage your SSH keys, which you'll need before connecting to the server. Continue to the next step.

What is Authentik?

Authentik is the identity system running behind auth.tharavad.xyz. It handles your account, password, and SSH public keys. Think of it as the "admin panel" for your tharavad identity — you'll return here whenever you need to add a new SSH key or update account details.
